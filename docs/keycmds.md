| --- | ------- |
, | Segment

/ | Haze
; | Skin
A | Auto Connect

C | Contact Point
D | Static Body
E | Select Point
F | Contact Force
G | Fog
H | Convex Hull
I | Inertia
J | Joint
K | Skybox
L | Additive
M | Center of Mass
N | ~~Constraint~~
O | Perturb Object
P | ~~Contact Split~~
Q | Camera
R | Reflection
S | ~~SCL Inertia~~
S | Shadow
T | Transparent
U | Actuator
V | Tendon
W | Wireframe
X | Texture
Y | ~~Range Finder~~
Z | ~~Light~~

### Simulator Commands
| Key  | Command |
| ---- | ------- |
Esc    | ~~free camera~~
Enter  | slow motion
Space  | pause
Right  | step forward
Left   | ~~step back~~
Down   | step forward 100
Up     | ~~step back 100~~
PgUp   | ~~previous keyreset~~
PgDn   | ~~next keyreset~~
BackSp | reset

### Robot Commands (Numeric Keypad)
| Key  | Command |
| ---- | ------- |
PgUp  | increase velocity
PgDn  | decrease velocity
Home  | increase height
End   | decrease height
<     | yaw to left
>     | yaw to right
^     | pitch nose up
down  | pitch nose down
/     | roll left
*     | roll right
-     | toggle activatation
+     | toggle trot
Enter | toggle hop

### Control Key Commands
| Key  | Command |
| ---- | ------- |
Ctrl A | align scale
Ctrl P | print quaternions
Ctrl Q | quit simulator
Ctrl V | toggles between recording and not recording video
